# SaveTube 

**Download any YouTube video to your device. No subscriptions. No limits. Yours forever.**

---

SaveTube is a personal offline video manager built for people who are tired of losing access to content — whether it's a deleted video, a trip with no signal, or just not wanting to burn mobile data rewatching the same thing.

Paste a YouTube link. Pick your quality. Done. The video lives on your device.

---

## Why SaveTube?

Most YouTube downloaders are sketchy websites drowning in ads, browser extensions that stop working every other week, or bloated desktop apps from 2012. SaveTube is different — it's a clean, lightweight tool you run yourself on your own machine. No third-party servers. No data collection. No nonsense.

- **Quality control** — choose from 4K, 1080p, 720p, 480p, 360p, or audio-only MP3
- **Real progress tracking** — live download speed, percentage, and ETA
- **Works offline** — once downloaded, no internet needed ever again
- **Mobile ready** — native Android app that saves directly to your phone's storage
- **Your data stays yours** — everything runs locally on your own PC

---

## How it works

SaveTube runs a lightweight Python backend on your PC using FastAPI and yt-dlp — the most reliable YouTube extraction library available. The frontend is a sleek mobile-style interface you can open in any browser. The Android app connects over your home WiFi and saves finished downloads straight to your phone.
